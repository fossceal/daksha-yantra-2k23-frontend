DAKSHA YANTHRA

Daksha Yanthra, the inter college techno cultural fest of College of Engineering Attingal throws open a stage for talents to battle it out and rise to glory. Daksha, the cultural fest presents a spectacle to the audience as musical notes, dance moves and thoughtful words amalgamated in the perfect proportion with gala produces an extravaganza. Yanthra, the technical fest being the battlefield for technical minds to compete blended with a fine balance of entertainment making it a day that one cannot afford to miss.
